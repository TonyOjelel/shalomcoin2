Buy BNB on an exchange (i.e. Binance, Gate, Coinbase etc.). Transfer the tokens to your MetaMask wallet address. BEP-20 addresses start with a "0x"

Head on to PancakeSwap and swap for $ShalomCoin

Click here to head on over to PancakeSwap or use this address) to select ShalomCoin. Set the slippage tolerance to 10% (sometimes it may be a bit more, depending on how much demand there is)
